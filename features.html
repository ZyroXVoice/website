ZyroX's features

Quick Disclaimer

ZyroX is in beta right now. A lot of these features are currently unavailable or under heavy development. Most of them aren't implemented in the beta right now. Do you have a suggestion? Join the Discord!

Voice controlled

ZyroX can be controlled with your voice. Yes, you read that right. Start off by saying "Hey ZyroX" and it'll listen for your voice. Of course, you can mute your microphone in the Settings tab.

Obfuscation on the go

Obfuscate your scripts on the go! Simply click the Obfuscate button or use the "obfuscate" voice command, and it's all handled from there! Alternatively, head over to our website and obfuscate it there.

In need of Scripts?

Just find some online! Open the script hub and start scouring the web - all from the ZyroX Application.

Feeling lonely? :(

Open up the chat and start socializing! Maybe make some new friends, share some cool scripts, or just mention your favorite color.
